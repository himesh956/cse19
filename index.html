A paragraph is a self-contained unit of writing that focuses on a single topic. It is composed of a group of related sentences that work together to develop one central idea. A well-structured paragraph typically includes a topic sentence that states the main idea, supporting sentences that provide details and evidence, and a concluding sentence that summarizes the main point.
